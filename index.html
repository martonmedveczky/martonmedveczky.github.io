Our story

We set out on this journey after spending years in the startup ecosystem: Andy as a founder and Marton as a VC.

During our time we were amazed to see how efficient startups could be and how easy they can replicate a large portion of what corporations do just because everyone in the team uses the latest technologies and fully understands how to use products/libraries/frameworks effectively.

We realized that this speed and efficiency is missing from top companies and that we can help to overcome the inertia that is prevalent among corporations.

How does this work?

It's pretty simple really.

  1. We have a discussion and you tell us what would be interesting to you.
  2. We go through our database and come up with potential targets that we think could be a good fit for you.
  3. We approach the targets, find out intent and work out an offer.
  4. We hold your hand during the acquisition process, work with lawyers and diligence providers, help you sign and close fast.
How long is the process?

Depends on a lot of factors but typically 6-9 months from start to finish.
